I don't go anywhere
  1. Get 2 slices of bread, peanut butter, jelly, plate and a butter knife.
  2. Place the 2 slices of bread on the plate.
  3. Use the butter knife to evenly spread a 1 cm thick layer of peanut butter on one of the slices of bread.
  4. Use the butter knife to evenly spread a 0.5 cm thick layer of jelly.
  5. Place the slices of bread on top of one another with the jelly side facing the peanut butter side.
